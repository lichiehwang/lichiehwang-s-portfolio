Mascot site

This website is dedicated to the cute characters of Kobe City,the goal is to make citizens feel more connected and have a deeper understanding of municipal activities.

URL

https://mascot-site

Role

Design and Coding

Purpose of the Site

To promote Kobe's mascots and, in turn, encourage tourism and exploration of the city.

Target

Residents of Kobe and tourists visiting the city

About the Design

The background color is chosen to be blue, symbolizing the concept of Kobe's mascot soaring in the sky. Paired with a cute design style, it imparts a sense of warmth and friendliness.
