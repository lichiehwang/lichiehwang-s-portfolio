Babyshop site

"aki select" is a website that provides a rental service for traditional Chinese baby celebration items. The brand name symbolizes a partnership with customers throughout the child's growth process. By offering adorable traditional items for baby celebrations, the goal is to assist customers in creating unique and wonderful memories.

URL

https://babyshop

Role

Design and Coding

Purpose of the Site

To raise awareness of the service and acquire new clients.

Target

Parents of newborns

About the Design

The owner, "aki," sounds like "秋" (autumn in Japanese). Therefore, the website adopts a serene color palette inspired by autumn. It combines gentle color blocks and line designs to create a soothing visual experience.
